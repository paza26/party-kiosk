# Party Kiosk - Your Modern Point of Sale System

**The simple, fast, and beautiful way to run your cafe, restaurant, or event.**

Party Kiosk is a point-of-sale system designed for businesses that need to take orders and process payments quickly - without complications. Whether you're running a busy cafe, food truck, bar, or hosting an event, Party Kiosk keeps you organized and your customers happy.

---

## What You Get

- **One-Touch Ordering** - Tap products to create orders in seconds. No typing. No confusion.
- **Instant Totals** - Customers see the price immediately. Built-in math that actually works.
- **Easy Payment** - Cash or card. Automatic change calculation included.
- **See Your Day** - Track today's revenue and order history at a glance.
- **Nothing to Lose** - Everything saves automatically. Your data is always there.
- **Works Everywhere** - Phone, tablet, desktop. Same app, any device.
- **Beautiful Product Setup** - Create your menu with emoji icons. No boring text lists.

## How It Works

### 1. Set Up Your Products (Once)
Open Party Kiosk and add your menu items:
- **Product name** - "Espresso", "Sandwich", "Mojito"
- **Pick an emoji** - Visual identity for quick recognition
- **Set the price** - What you want to charge
- **Choose a category** - Organize by type (drinks, food, desserts)

Done. Your menu is ready.

### 2. Take an Order (Fast)
Customer walks up. You tap the screen:
- **Tap products** - Espresso, Sandwich, Espresso. Each tap adds to the order.
- **See the total** - Real-time calculation. No surprises.
- **Remove items** - Tap again if you made a mistake.
- **Adjust quantities** - Need 2 espressos? Easy.

### 3. Process Payment (Simple)
- **Cash?** - Tell Party Kiosk the payment amount. It calculates change.
- **Card?** - Record it and move on.
- **Done** - Order is saved automatically.

### 4. Check Your Day (Smart)
At the end of the day (or anytime):
- See total revenue
- Browse all orders
- Check which products sold best
- No manual counting needed
